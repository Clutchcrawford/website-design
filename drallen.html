MEET THE DOCTOR-DR.ALLEN

Dr.Rickey Allen was born in Natchez,Mississippi and raised in Zachary,Louisiana.He has lived in the Metropolitan Atlanta Area since 1993.Furthermore, Dr.Allen is a Georgia Board Certified chiropractor.

Before recieving his certification in chiropractic field,Dr. Allen recieved his Masters of Clinical Pastoral Counseling from Revelation Message Bible College in Jacksonville, Florida. A devout christian, he is also recognized as being licenced with advanced standing with the National Christian Counseling Association (NCCA).

Dr.Allen is also a married to Mrs.Lynne Allen. Lynne works in the office with Dr.Allen. Dr. Allen and Lynne were married in Baton Rouge, Louisiana and have been married since 1975.
